Haflati Brand Guidelines

The complete brand identity system for Saudi Arabia's premier event planning marketplace. Version 1.0 — March 2026.

1. Brand Foundation

The strategic core that drives every brand decision.

Brand Purpose

To make every Saudi celebration effortless and extraordinary by connecting people with the right event professionals.

Brand Vision

To become the trusted standard for event planning across Saudi Arabia and the wider Gulf region, where every occasion is planned with confidence and joy.

Brand Mission

Haflati connects Saudi consumers with verified event service providers through a transparent, bilingual marketplace that makes discovering, comparing, and booking event services simple.

Brand Values

ValueExpression
Trust (ثقة)Verified vendors, transparent pricing, authentic reviews. Every interaction builds confidence.
Joy (فرح)Celebrations should be exciting, not stressful. Our brand radiates warmth and positivity.
Simplicity (بساطة)Complex planning made effortless. Clear interfaces, straightforward processes.
Heritage (أصالة)Rooted in Saudi culture and traditions while embracing modern global standards.
Community (مجتمع)We strengthen the ecosystem for both consumers and vendors, growing together.

Brand Personality

Haflati speaks like a knowledgeable friend who loves celebrations — warm but not casual, professional but never cold, modern but respectful of tradition. Think: the friend everyone calls when they need to plan an event because they always know the best vendors.

Brand Promise

"Your celebration, perfectly planned." — Every vendor is verified, every price is transparent, every experience is curated for excellence.

2. Logo System

The Haflati mark is built around a four-pointed celebration sparkle enclosed in a rounded square. The sparkle represents the moment of joy at the heart of every event. Surrounding smaller sparkles convey energy and festivity. A subtle decorative arc at the base references the Arabic letter Ha (ح), grounding the mark in its Arabic identity.

Logo Variants

Primary Logo Icon + Arabic + English. Use for hero sections, presentations, print materials.
Stacked Logo Vertical layout. Use for splash screens, app stores, centered placements.
Horizontal Logo (Light BG) For navigation bars, headers, email signatures. Navy English text.
Horizontal Logo (Dark BG) For dark backgrounds. White English text, gradient Arabic text.
App Icon For app icons, social media avatars, favicons.
Icon (Dark Background) Dark variant with gradient sparkle on navy background.

Clear Space and Minimum Sizes

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the icon mark on all sides. This ensures the logo breathes and remains legible in every context.

VariantMinimum WidthUse Case
Primary Logo200px / 50mmHero sections, print headers
Stacked Logo160px / 40mmSplash screens, centered placements
Horizontal Logo140px / 35mmNavigation bars, email signatures
Icon Only32px / 8mmFavicons, small UI elements
App Icon48px / 12mmApp stores, home screen

Logo Usage Rules

Do

  • Use provided SVG files without modification
  • Maintain the aspect ratio when scaling
  • Use the dark variant on backgrounds darker than 50% gray
  • Use the mono white version over photographs or busy backgrounds
  • Place on solid or very subtle backgrounds
  • Ensure minimum clear space is maintained

Do Not

  • Rotate, skew, or distort the logo in any way
  • Change the gradient colors or direction
  • Add shadows, outlines, or effects to the logo
  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Rearrange the icon and text elements
  • Use below minimum size specifications
  • Recreate or approximate the logo with other elements
  • Animate the core sparkle mark (sparkle accents may be animated)

6. Iconography and Imagery

Visual language beyond the logo.

Icon Style

  • Use rounded, friendly line icons (2px stroke weight at 24px size)
  • Corner radius: 2px minimum on all icon shapes
  • Default color: Navy (#16213E) on light backgrounds, White on dark backgrounds
  • Active/selected state: Rose (#E8636B) or brand gradient
  • Recommended icon set: Lucide Icons or Phosphor Icons (both open source, rounded style)

Photography Guidelines

  • Show real Saudi celebrations featuring diverse people (families, couples, professionals)
  • Warm, natural lighting preferred over harsh studio lighting
  • Color grading should lean warm (complementing rose and gold palette)
  • Show genuine emotion: joy, laughter, connection, pride
  • Represent Saudi culture authentically: traditional and modern celebrations
  • Include vendor professionals at work (photographers shooting, decorators setting up)
  • Avoid stock photos that feel generic or non-Saudi

Illustration Style

  • Flat or semi-flat illustration with subtle gradients
  • Use brand color palette exclusively
  • Geometric, clean shapes with rounded edges
  • Include celebration elements: sparkles, confetti dots, gentle curves
  • Cultural motifs: geometric Islamic patterns as subtle background textures (never as primary elements)

RTL Layout Notes

  • The app is Arabic-first: default direction is RTL
  • Use CSS logical properties: margin-inline-start instead of margin-left
  • Icon arrows and directional elements must flip in RTL
  • Numbers and English text within Arabic context remain LTR (handled automatically by Unicode bidi algorithm)
  • Logo does not flip in RTL — it remains in its designed orientation

8. Brand Protection

Safeguarding brand integrity and intellectual property.

Trademark Strategy

  • Register "Haflati" and "حفلتي" as trademarks with the Saudi Authority for Intellectual Property (SAIP)
  • Register the sparkle logo mark as a device mark
  • File in Nice Classification classes: 35 (advertising/business), 42 (technology services), 38 (telecommunications)
  • Secure domain variations: haflati.com, haflati.sa, haflati.app (already owned)
  • Register on social platforms: consistent @haflati.app or @Haflati_app handles

Brand Compliance Monitoring

  • Conduct quarterly brand audits across all touchpoints (app, website, social, print)
  • Maintain a shared brand asset library (Google Drive or Figma) with current approved files only
  • All external vendors and partners must receive the brand guidelines before creating any materials
  • Designate a brand guardian within the team who approves all public-facing brand usage
  • Set up Google Alerts for "Haflati" and "حفلتي" to monitor unauthorized usage

Co-Branding Rules

  • Partner logos must never be larger than the Haflati logo
  • Maintain clear separation between Haflati branding and partner branding
  • Never merge or combine the Haflati logo with another brand's logo
  • Partner color palettes must not replace Haflati brand colors in joint materials
